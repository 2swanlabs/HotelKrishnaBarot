Our Story

A Hidden Gem
in the Himalayas

Nestled alongside the emerald waters of Barot Valley, Hotel Krishna is a sanctuary where the Himalayan wilderness becomes your backyard. Wake to the river's sound, have breakfast with mountain views, and fall asleep beneath a sky full of stars.

Located on Temple Road near the historic fountain, we blend warm Himachali hospitality with all modern comforts — inviting you to slow down and truly arrive.
